Welcome to Camp Blue Ridge

Explore All 4 Seasons

Are you looking for a family getaway, a church group retreat or youth group adventure? Then look no further! Nestled in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains, our cozy cabins offer the perfect solution for families and groups to relax and experience the best that the Blue Ridge has to offer!
Hike miles of trails to scenic waterfalls, enjoy a picnic or go canoeing and fishing right here at camp!
Explore the local area and enjoy fresh seasonal fruit from nearby orchards or visit numerous historical sites and enjoy delicious farm to table fair in Lexington.
The options are endless and we are here to help you make each getaway an unforgettable experience!
